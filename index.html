About inSTA

Indian Social Trusted Application

inSTA, short for Indian Social Trusted Application, is a next-generation social networking platform designed to empower India's voice in the global digital space. Unlike foreign-owned apps, inSTA is built with India-first values, prioritizing cultural inclusivity, user trust, and data sovereignty.

Our mission is simple – to give Indians a social media that celebrates identity, connects communities, and protects privacy. Whether it's sharing stories, engaging in discussions, or celebrating traditions, inSTA is designed to be more than an app – it's a movement.

🛡️ Trust First

Built with privacy and security at its core, ensuring your data stays in India.

🇮🇳 Cultural Pride
